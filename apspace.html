University Study Space Reservation Web Application With Indoor Mapping (Student Information System)

Enhancing the existing student information system (APSpace) of Asia Pacific University of Technology & Innovation (APU) by integrating indoor mapping capabilities and study space reservation features. This enhancement aims to offer students the convenience of locating university facilities and reserving study spaces seamlessly. Through detailed indoor mapping of the university layout and the addition of study space reservation features, students can efficiently navigate the campus and secure study spaces according to their preferences.

The primary features in this system are:

  1. University facility exploration
    • View indoor map and near real-time availability of university facilities
    • Search and filter available university facilities by various criteria (time, power sockets, air-conditioning, seating capacity, name)
  2. Reservation management
    • Reserve study spaces
    • View study space reservations
    • Cancel study space reservations
    • Check in to study space reservations using QR code authentication
    • Check out of study space reservations
    • Extend study space reservations
  3. Indoor map management
    • Create and manage drawing packages, conversions, datasets, and tilesets of indoor map using Azure API
    • Configure default indoor map
  4. University facility management
    • Create and manage different types of university facilities (classroom, tech lab, auditorium, study tables, library rooms)
    • Download QR codes of university facilities
  5. Data and analytics
    • View analytics of peak usage and utilization rates of study spaces
