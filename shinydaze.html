Home Cleaning Service Web Application

The home cleaning industry is experiencing a notable surge in demand for efficient and streamlined service delivery. Manual booking and scheduling processes are not only time-consuming but also susceptible to errors, often resulting in issues such as double bookings, missed appointments, and inefficient allocation of cleaning staff. In response to these challenges, the developed system is dedicated to simplifying the booking and scheduling of cleaning services, catering to three primary user roles: member, cleaner, and administrator.

Member

  • Check slot availability and book cleaning service
  • View booking history to view assigned cleaner, provide feedback, or cancel booking
  • View profile and update profile information

Cleaner

  • View assigned job and update job status
  • Apply leave and view leave application status

Administrator

  • Manage cleaner
  • Manage cleaning service
  • Manage service area
  • Manage booking
  • Manage leave application
