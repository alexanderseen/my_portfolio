Home Workout Mobile Application

During the Covid-19 pandemic, the disruption of outdoor activities and gym closures led to the development of APWorkout. This app serves as a virtual fitness solution, enabling users to exercise safely and effectively at home. Through interactive features and personalized guidance, users are motivated to maintain a consistent fitness routine.

The primary features in this system are:

  1. Workout routine list
    • Access diverse home workout routines tailored for different fitness levels, goals, and preferences
    • Detailed instructions, sets, reps, and videos ensure proper form and technique
  2. Todo list
    • Record completed tasks and monitor fitness progress for motivation.
  3. BMI calculator
    • Accurately calculate BMI by inputting height and weight
    • Get immediate feedback on BMI and healthy ranges
